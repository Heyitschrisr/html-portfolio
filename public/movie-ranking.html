The Best Movies According to Chris

My Top 3 Movies of All time

Spiderman 2(2004)

This is a sequel to Spiderman from 2002. This movie holds a speical place
in my heart. I have always been a spiderman fan and to me this is the perfect
spiderman movie. This movie makes me feel like anything is posssible and
that we all have a hero inside of us.

Ferris's Buellers Day Off(1986)

This is a movie from the 80's. This movie gives me a sense of freedom and
confidence to attack the world stree free.

The Social Network(2010)

This is a movie about Mark Zuckerburg the creator of Facebook. It is a dramatization
of how Facebook was made but, my take away from this movie is how someone
can create something so big and mainstream with an idea and his skills on a computer.
